Privacy Policy

This page explains how HowCanIHack.com handles basic analytics, logs, and limited technical data.

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

HowCanIHack.com is an educational website focused on cybersecurity and ethical hacking. We aim to collect only the minimum amount of information necessary to keep the site running, understand traffic, and improve content.

Server Logs

Like most websites, our hosting provider may automatically collect standard web server logs. These typically include:

  • IP address and basic network information
  • Browser type and version
  • Referring page and visited URLs
  • Date and time of each request

Logs are used for debugging, security monitoring, and capacity planning. We do not attempt to personally identify visitors from these logs unless required to investigate abuse or security incidents.

Analytics and Cookies

We may use privacy‑respecting analytics or third‑party services (such as Google Analytics) to understand aggregate traffic patterns. These tools may use cookies or similar technologies.

Where applicable, you can control cookies through your browser settings or built‑in consent options offered by the analytics provider.

Third‑Party Content

Some pages may embed content or scripts from third parties, such as:

  • Advertising networks (e.g., Google AdSense)
  • Analytics providers
  • Code snippet platforms or media CDNs

These services may collect their own usage data according to their privacy policies. Please refer to the respective provider’s documentation for details.

Contact and User Data

At this time, HowCanIHack.com does not operate user accounts or store sensitive personal data directly on the site. If you contact us via email or other channels, we will receive the information you choose to share and will use it solely to respond or handle your request.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the site and any limited data we do handle, but no system is perfectly secure. Never send passwords, highly sensitive personal data, or confidential information through contact forms or emails related to this site.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding access, correction, or deletion of personal data. Because we store very little information directly, most requests can be handled by clearing cookies, adjusting browser settings, or contacting us if you have specific concerns.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, regulations, or how the site operates. The “Last updated” date will be revised accordingly.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, you can reach the site owner or maintainer using the contact details referenced in our legal disclaimer or via the project’s public repository.
