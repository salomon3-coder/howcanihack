Contact

Need to report an error, request a correction, suggest a topic, or discuss collaboration? This page explains the best channel for each request.

Best way to reach us

Preferred channel: GitHub Issues on the public repository.

This helps us track requests transparently and resolve fixes faster.

Repository: github.com/salomon3-coder/howcanihack

What to include in your message

  • URL of the page or article
  • What is incorrect, unclear, or outdated
  • Suggested correction with source links (if possible)
  • Any screenshots/log output that helps reproduce the issue

Response expectations

We review messages in batches. High-impact issues (factual errors, broken links, security-sensitive mistakes) are prioritized first.

Security and legal note

Do not send illegal requests or ask for unauthorized attack instructions. All content and communications are handled under an ethical, defensive-use policy.
